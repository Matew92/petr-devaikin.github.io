Augmented Reality Furniture Catalogue

Together with Matteo Lever we designed and implemented a prototype of an augmented reality furniture catalogue for Augmented Reality and Tangible Interfaces course at Paris-Sud University. Each furniture item has a special sticky marker placed on the same page. Users can point a camera of their phones to the marker to see a 3d model of the item and interact with it. Markers can be easily removed from pages and put together to create a model of interior.

Interaction Points

Point a phone camera to a marker to see a 3d model of a furniture item.

Select an item by tapping on it.
Choose one of the color and material options.
Rotate an item on 90 degrees by a flickering gesture.
Move a camera closer to an item to see details.
Put a few markers together to immitate a room.
