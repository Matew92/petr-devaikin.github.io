Augmented Reality Furniture Catalogue

Together with Matteo Lever we designed and implemented a prototype of an augmented reality furniture catalogue for
Augmented Reality and Tangible Interfaces course at Paris-Sud University. All items in the magazine are presented with sticky markers. Users can use their mobile phones to interact with 3d models of furniture.

Interaction Points

Point a phone camera to a marker to see a 3d model of a furniture item.

Select an item by tapping.

Choose one of colors and materials.

Rotate an item by a flickering gesture.
Move a camera closer to an item to see details.
Put a few markers together to immitate a room.
