  • Kevin Perez

Æ–movement is an interactive system for real-time visualization of body performance.

Our goal is to emphasize uniqueness and temporality of the movement by immersing users in full body aesthetic experience.

Working Principle

  • Capture the Movement

    Accelerometer and gyroscope data is collected by a mobile phone attached to a participant's ankle or wrist.

  • Analyse the Data

    Captured data is transfered from the mobile phone to a computer in real-time and filtered to reduce noise.

  • Visualize

    Mapping different movements to different visual variables we create a unique visual representation of participants' body expression.
