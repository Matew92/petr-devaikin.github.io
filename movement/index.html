  • Petr Devaikin
  • Iva Karabatić
  • Kevin Perez

Our idea was to design an interactive system which emphasizes uniqueness and temporality of the body movement.

Working Principle

  • Capture the Movement

    We attached a mobile phone to a participant's ankle or wrist and get the accelerometer and gyroscope data

  • Analyse the Data

    Data was transfered to a computer and filtered in real-time

  • Visualize

    Data is mapped to visual representation
